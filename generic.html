Alpha Transportation Systems Team

At Alpha Transportation Systems Inc, we specialize in the safe, efficient, and timely transportation of lumber and other essential freight across North Carolina and beyond. Based in Concord, North Carolina, our company is proud to be an active carrier serving the region with dedication and reliability.

While our core focus is on lumber hauling, we also transport general freight, motor vehicles, and more. We are committed to maintaining a modern fleet, upholding the highest safety standards, and supporting our team members with respect and integrity.

We are always looking for passionate and skilled individuals from CDL-A licensed drivers to dispatchers and logistics personnel who are ready to grow their careers in the transportation industry.

  • Competitive pay and comprehensive benefits
  • Up-to-date, well-maintained fleet and equipment
  • Dedicated to safety, reliability, and on-time delivery
  • Family-oriented company culture
  • Opportunities for professional growth and advancement
